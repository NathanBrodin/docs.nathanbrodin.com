# Setting up your Web Browser
In this section, I'll share my experience in configuring my web browser, including the extensions I use, and my favorite bookmarks.

## Extensions
To enhance my browsing experience, I installed the following extensions from the [Chrome web store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/category/extensions?hl=fr/)
- Bitwarden
- Blocktube -> Disable Youtube shorts
- reMarkable
- Wikiwand
- Sponsorblock
- Money converter
